This language is, like many esolangs, a very cruel joke. 
In addition to the Turing tar-pit semantics and bizarre computing abstraction,
and in fact, *far* more important than them are:
1. "set" is maximally polysemous
2. both human and LLM tokenizers have a tendency to collapse similar items under stress

Minor features:
* Every possible tradeoff that a useful language would make, has been made:
   1. in the opposite direction,
   2. ignored, or
   3. been replaced with a lose/lose situation.
* Exponential reasoning difficulty
* Whitespace-agnostic
   * but go ahead and indent whatever you like
* No bitwise capability
* No integer arithmetic[\*](./ARITHMETIC.md)
   * the language is, however, based loosely on the fundamental definition of number.
